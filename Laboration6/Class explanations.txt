Point2D - Used to give both the player and the enemies their position.

GameObject - Only really used to keep screen size and character size in a place accessible to both player and enemy.

shape - Used by circle to give both players and enemies their color.

circle - Used to give the player and the enemies their shape.

enemy - Renders, gives speed to the enemies. Also gives collison between enemies and walls.

player - Renders and gives mobility to the player. Also makes the player have collision with walls and enemies.
